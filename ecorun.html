EcoRun: 2022.08.07

Issue!

1.1 billion people lack access to clean water, 2.4 billion face inadequate sanitation problems, and 2 million children die due to diarrheal diseases, according to the WWF. Similarly, climate change has made this global issue even worse; rivers and lakes with access to fresh water are drying up, causing children to walk even further for access to fresh water for their families. As a result, we intend to bring awareness of this global issue through our 5K ECO-Run.

Our Approach

Plus X hosted the 5-kilometer ECO-run with a total of 27 participants at Banpo sports complex. Prior to the event, each sponsor pledged to support three participants with 100,000 won each if they covered 5 kilometers, the typical distance walked by children from countries in significant water crises in search of clean water. Despite the scorching weather, all young participants of diverse backgrounds managed to complete the walkathon in hopes of promoting the environment.

The ECO-run was a crucial event to the success of our entire project. With everyone supporting a single goal, the successful completion of this event proved the determination and perseverance of our youth for a better world. It granted our group with funds for many current and future projects and the participants with the knowledge, awareness, and motivation for further action.
