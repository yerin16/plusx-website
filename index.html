About Us

About Us

Plus X is a student-led non-profit organization determined to create meaningful change in our society through empowering youth to take action. We believe that with collaborative effort, we can come together to multiply our positive impact on the public for the betterment of all. By primarily focusing on the environment, we aim to raise public awareness of significant and prevalent environmental issues in the domestic pharmaceutical industry and provide helpful products to developing countries in urgent need of aid.

Projects

Eco-Run

With the help of our own members and young supporters, the 5-kilometer ECO-run drew in sponsorships to provide funds for many of our subsequent events such as the medication disposal campaign and donations to Oxfam.

Pharmacy Campaign

We focused on setting up pamphlets to alarm the public of the consequences of improper medication disposal. We aim to highly encourage the complete consumption of prescribed medicine, with further additional information.
